Who are we?

We are are Website made to show, report and inform the dangers of crashes in the the area. Here you can view both the factors of the crash and where it took place in Queensland.

Factors List

This website has a list of crashes involving factors within the crash. To see this, click on "Factors" button and then click on which crash ID you would like to see.

Locations List

This website also has a second list of crashes involving their locations within the crash. To see this, click on "Locations" button and then click on which crash ID you would like to see.

Damage Factoring

Crashes have been devided into four main sections based on their damage. These are listed bellow


  • Minor

  • Minor class is where there are zero fatalities and little to no medical attentions. This includes Property Damage and Minor Treatment

  • Mid-range

  • Mid-range class involves medical attention that has little to no fatality rate. This includes Medical Attention and Hospitalisation

  • Major

  • Major class involes any form of fatality rate and Hospitalisation above 100.
