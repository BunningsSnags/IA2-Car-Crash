Damage Factoring

Crashes have been devided into four main sections based on their damage. These are listed bellow


  • Minor

  • Minor class is where there are zero fatalities and little to no medical attentions. This includes Property Damage and Minor Treatment

  • Mid-range

  • Mid-range class involves medical attention that has little to no fatality rate. This includes Medical Attention and Hospitalisation

  • Major

  • Major class involves fatality rates that are not above 30 or hospitals rates that are above 100. This includes Fatal, Hospitalisation and Medical Treatment

  • Extreme

  • Extreme class is where there is a fatality rate above 30 and is classed as a Fatal severity
